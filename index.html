Classic Stacker!
This is a game to test your reflexes. The bar will go from left to right and back until you press the spacebar to drop the bar. You want to stack the blocks as high as you can without missing the blocks you stacked from the bottom up. This will get smaller and faster as you go up. The goal of the game is to get up to the gold bar for big prize. This game is so fun to play and will be unblocked in Mr. Mumm's app creations class. For you to start playing just click the start button. If you want to play again press start, or click the refresh button if you want to refresh the whole game. Good Luck
